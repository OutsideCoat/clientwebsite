Publications & Research

Art, science, and storytelling in print and on campus.

From illustrating student media to presenting research on geology and upcycled art, Maya shares visual narratives that connect science, sustainability, and community spaces.

Overview

Where to find the work.

Maya presented her research on the art-science connection in North Carolina geology at the Charlotte Undergraduate Research Conference in 2025 and on the impact of upcycled materials on art at the PIT Journal Conference in 2023.
