About Me

I am Maya Hutagalung, a Charlotte-based artist.

Maya Hutagalung is a student pursuing a B.F.A in Illustration with a minor in Earth and Environmental Science. She is interested in the intersections between humans and nature and using art to communicate these connections.

Maya has interned with The Xerces Society for Invertebrate Conservation and Visit Lake Norman, creating published materials including scientific illustrations, social media campaigns, signage, and commercial products.
